Zuppa Toscana

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced or 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 c chicken broth
  • 2 c water
  • 6 medium Yukon Gold potatoes, washed and sliced into thin discs or russet potatoes
  • 2 c chopped fresh kale, stems removed (or spinach)
  • 1 c heavy whipping cream (or half and half)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Grated Parmesan, to taste
  • 6 pieces bacon, cooked and crumbled (or 1/3 c real bacon bits)

Stovetop Instructions

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat, brown the sausage until cooked through. Remove from pot and set aside on paper towels.
  2. Add diced onion to the pot, and sauté until translucent. Add minced garlic and sauté 1 minute.
  3. Add broth and water, and bring to a boil. Once boiling, add potatoes and reduce heat to a rolling simmer. Cook 10-14 minutes or until fork tender.
  4. Add your kale, sausage, and cream, and bring back to a rolling simmer. When kale is tender, remove from heat.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper, then garnish with bacon and Parmesan to serve! (Tip: add a few shakes of red pepper flakes at the end for a little spice)

Slow Cooker Instructions

  1. Follow steps 1-2 as instructed above.
  2. Next, add sausage, onion mixture, broth, water and potatoes to a slow cooker. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if desired) to taste. Cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours or High for 3 to 4 hours.
  3. Pour in heavy cream, and stir in kale to combine. Cover and cook on high until flavors are incorporated, about 30 minutes more.
  4. Garnish with bacon and Parmesan cheese, and enjoy!

Notes

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Ready in: 30 minutes

Serves: 6
